TASKS

Walk, Cycling, or take Public Transports

The majority of the vehicles on the world's roads burn either diesel or petrol. Driving less and using a bike or walking will both improve our health and fitness and benefit the environment. Consider using a bus or train for larger trips.

Task-

Instead of driving a car or bike, the participants must walk or ride a bicycle for the required amount of time over a shorter distance. Additionally, they will use public transportation for larger distances. Participants will receive rewards if they complete the task's requirements in full.

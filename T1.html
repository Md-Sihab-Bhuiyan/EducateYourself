TASKS

Tree plantation and gardening

In this task, the participant will be asked to plant trees. There will be some given target if the participant is able to fulfil the target properly, then the person will get the reward. In the city, one person can do gardening on the rooftop or on the balcony. And also one person can plant trees in an open field.

Task-

The task will ask the participant to plant a number of trees and after the plantation, there will be an allocated duration to nurturing those trees. The participant will be asked for pictures or other evidence that actually shows he/she did the work properly.

If we can turn all rooftops and balconies into gardens, it will have a huge impact on our environment.

We can make the world better.
